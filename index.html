Cats vs. Dogs is a fun little game where you play as a cat, and you must fight off the invading dog army throughout the levels of the game.


The planet Felis is currently being invaded by armies of robot dogs! They have been taking over the planet and terrorizing the local felines. With all of the cats' best efforts failing, all seems lost. You are now the last hope the cats have. It is now up to you to find a way to stop the dogs' plans and save the planet!
